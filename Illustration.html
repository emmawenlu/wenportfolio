Memes are a fusion of words + images.

In this project, we need to create typophoto posters with custom typography and your own imagery.
We cannot use any pre-existing fonts (we need to create a customized font using FontStruct
We cannot use any pre-existing imagery but we can get photos through computer, phone, etc, draw, scan, or collage.
And we cannot use color, this work must be black + white.

This project require us try making posters that would be moving enough to make someone cry. In my view, everybody has their two sides, strong and week. Break through the worldly concepts and be the unique one.
The theme of this assignment revolves around touching. Homesickness is the sad plot of every person who leaves their hometown and struggles elsewhere, and wants to convey the feeling of longing.
What touches people's hearts is killing and separation. Everything has their aura.
Mankind is progressing and science and technology are advanced. However, the ruthless destruction of nature, reclaiming and harming animals.

Personal Sigil

This project, we need to research the history of a group. It could be our family, a fictional family, or a close personal friends.
Use that research to create a coat of arms-style collection of symbols in a vectorized form; we would refer to this as a sigil.
After we finish sigils, also need to do 3 mockups.
This Sigil inspires by my hometown-Qingdao where I was born from. It is a beautiful costal city with the sea on three sides
has abundant maritime resources. Dolphin symbolizes peace, smart and be nice to people.
You can see there are couple icons surround the outline They are characters of a dynasty and it means different weather.
Weather could be represent different personalities of people.
That's why I use these elements to be my family member
